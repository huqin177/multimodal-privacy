Claire Dubois
French Pastry Chef

Biography

Claire Dubois was born and raised in Paris, where her love for pastry and baking blossomed at an early age. She comes from a long line of bakers, and her family's bakery, "La Boulangerie Dubois," has been a cherished institution in their neighborhood for generations. Claire's passion for creating delightful pastries and desserts led her to pursue a formal education in culinary arts at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris, where she honed her skills under the guidance of renowned pastry chefs.

Education

- Le Cordon Bleu, Paris, France - Diploma in Pastry Arts

Work Experience

- Owner and Head Pastry Chef, Délices Parisiens (Current)
- Pastry Chef, La Boulangerie Dubois (Former)

Hobby

Apart from her love for baking, Claire is an avid collector of vintage pastry cookbooks. She enjoys exploring antique bookshops and discovering forgotten recipes and techniques from the past.
