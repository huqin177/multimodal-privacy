Biography

Kwame Jok Aken is a dedicated educator and researcher with a passion for promoting higher education in South Sudan. Born and raised in Juba, he has always been deeply connected to his community and is committed to contributing to its development through academia.

Education Experience

  • Bachelor of Science in Education from the University of Juba
  • Master of Arts in History from the University of Nairobi
  • Ph.D. in African Studies from the University of Cape Town

Work Experience

  • Lecturer at the University of Juba, Department of History and Cultural Studies, for over 10 years.
  • Published several research papers on South Sudanese history and culture.
