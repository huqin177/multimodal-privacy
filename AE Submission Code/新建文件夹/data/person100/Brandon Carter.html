Bio

Hello! My name is Brandon Carter, and I'm an Environmental Scientist working with GreenFuture Inc. With a deep passion for our planet, I have spent the past five years dedicated to climate change research. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science from Evergreen State College. My work has been published in several scientific journals, contributing to our understanding of sustainable practices.

When I'm not immersed in research, I enjoy wildlife photography and exploring the great outdoors through hiking. My photography has been featured in local galleries, and I'm always excited to share the beauty of our natural world with others.
