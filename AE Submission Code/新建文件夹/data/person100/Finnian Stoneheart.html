About Dr. Finnian Stoneheart

Dr. Finnian Stoneheart is an Assistant Professor specializing in Environmental Science at the University of Malaysia Pahang. He is known for his research on sustainable agriculture practices in tropical regions.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Environmental Science, University of Oxford
  • M.S. in Environmental Studies, Stanford University
  • B.S. in Biology, University of California, Berkeley

Work Experience

  • Assistant Professor, University of Malaysia Pahang
  • Research Fellow, Center for Sustainable Agriculture, International Institute of Environmental Studies
  • Environmental Consultant, GreenEarth Solutions
