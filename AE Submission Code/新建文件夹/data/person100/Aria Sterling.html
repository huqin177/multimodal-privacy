About Me

I am a Full Professor at the University of Hong Kong, specializing in the field of Economics. I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from the London School of Economics and Political Science, and I have dedicated my career to research and teaching in this fascinating discipline.

Research Interests

My research interests primarily revolve around Macroeconomics, International Trade, and Economic Development. I am passionate about exploring the complexities of these areas and contributing to our understanding of the global economy.
