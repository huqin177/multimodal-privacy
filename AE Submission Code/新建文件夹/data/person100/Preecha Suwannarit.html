Biography

Preecha Suwannarit was born and raised in Bangkok, Thailand. He has always had a passion for education and a deep love for his country's culture and traditions. After completing his undergraduate studies in Education at Chulalongkorn University, Preecha pursued a Master's degree in Educational Psychology at Mahidol University. His dedication to academics and research led him to earn a Ph.D. in Education from Mahasarakham University.

Education Experience

  • Bachelor's in Education from Chulalongkorn University
  • Master's in Educational Psychology from Mahidol University
  • Ph.D. in Education from Mahasarakham University

Work Experience

Preecha Suwannarit is currently an Assistant Professor at Mahasarakham University, where he specializes in Educational Psychology. He has published several research papers and is known for his innovative teaching methods.
