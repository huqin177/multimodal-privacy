Bio

Hello! I'm Julia Peterson, a passionate research assistant at UCLA, specializing in ecology and evolutionary biology. My academic journey began with a B.Sc. in Biology, and I'm currently pursuing my M.Sc. in the same field. I've had the opportunity to work as an intern at the California Academy of Sciences and as a lab assistant in the Life Sciences Department at UCLA. When I'm not immersed in research, I enjoy nature photography, hiking in the California wilderness, and bird watching. Feel free to reach out to me for any collaborations or discussions!

Research

My research focuses on the impact of urban development on local ecosystems and how species adapt to changing environments. I'm particularly interested in the conservation of native bird species and the ecological significance of urban green spaces.
