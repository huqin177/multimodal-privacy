Welcome to Eszter Nagy's Homepage

Biography

Eszter Nagy is a highly respected political science professor at the National University of Public Service in Hungary. She is known for her expertise in international relations and has published several influential research papers and books in her field. Eszter is a dedicated educator who is passionate about teaching and mentoring the next generation of political scientists. She is also actively involved in academic conferences and debates, contributing to the intellectual discourse on global politics.

Education Experience

  • Ph.D. in Political Science from Eötvös Loránd University
  • Master's Degree in International Relations from Central European University
  • Bachelor's Degree in Political Science from Budapest University of Technology and Economics
