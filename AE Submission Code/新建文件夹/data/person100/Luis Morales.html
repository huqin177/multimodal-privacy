About Me

Luis Morales is a passionate historian dedicated to the study of Central American history. Born and raised in San José, Costa Rica, he has a strong commitment to sharing his knowledge and love for the subject with his students and colleagues. With a focus on the colonial period, Luis has made significant contributions to the field through his research and teaching.

Education

  • Master's Degree in History from the University of Costa Rica
  • Ph.D. in History from a prestigious international university
