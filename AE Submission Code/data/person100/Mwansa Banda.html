About Prof. Mwansa Banda

Prof. Mwansa Banda is a dedicated and passionate professor at Kopaline University, specializing in Economics. Born and raised in Lusaka, Zambia, Mwansa has always had a deep love for education and has made significant contributions to the academic landscape of Zambia.

Education Experience

  • Ph.D. in Economics from a prestigious UK university
  • Master's degree in Economics from a well-regarded institution in South Africa
  • Bachelor's degree in Economics from the University of Zambia

Work Experience

  • Professor of Economics at Kopaline University for over a decade
  • Published several research papers on topics related to economic development in Zambia
  • Served as a consultant for various government agencies on economic policy matters
