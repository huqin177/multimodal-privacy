Lucas Bennett

Post-Doctoral Researcher at Stony Brook University

Biography

Lucas Bennett is a post-doctoral researcher specializing in the intersection of artificial intelligence and neuroscience. His work focuses on developing advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze and model neural data.
