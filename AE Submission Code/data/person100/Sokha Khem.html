Biography

Sokha Khem was born and raised in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. She is a passionate historian dedicated to preserving Cambodia's rich cultural heritage and history.

Education

  • Bachelor of Arts in History from the University of Phnom Penh
  • Master's Degree in Southeast Asian Studies from the National Institute of Cambodia
  • Ph.D. in History from the Royal Academy of Cambodia

Work Experience

  • Professor of History at the Royal Academy of Cambodia for 15 years
  • Published several research papers and books on Cambodian history and culture
  • Served as a consultant to UNESCO on cultural preservation projects in Cambodia
