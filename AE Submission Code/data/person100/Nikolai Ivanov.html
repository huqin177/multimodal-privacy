About Me

I am Nikolai Ivanov, a passionate professor at the University of Forestry in Sofia, Bulgaria. My love for nature and forestry has been a driving force in my life.

I was born and raised in Sofia and have spent many summers exploring the beautiful Bulgarian mountains, fostering a deep connection to the environment.

I have had the privilege of teaching at the University of Forestry for over 20 years. My expertise lies in forest ecology and sustainable forestry practices. I am also actively involved in various environmental conservation projects across Bulgaria.
