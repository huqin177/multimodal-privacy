About Me

Marco Rossi is an Assistant Professor at the University of Cagliari's Department of History. He has a passion for research and teaching, with a focus on Italian history and culture. Marco was born and raised in Cagliari, Italy, and is known for his friendly and approachable personality.

Education

  • Ph.D. in History from the University of Cagliari
  • Master's degree in Italian Renaissance Studies from the University of Florence
  • Bachelor's degree in History from the University of Bologna

Hobbies

In his free time, Marco enjoys exploring the historical sites of Italy, especially ancient ruins and Renaissance-era landmarks. He is an avid collector of rare books and documents related to Italian history and art.
