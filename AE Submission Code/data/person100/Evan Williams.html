Welcome to Prof. Evan Williams' Homepage

Biography

Evan Williams is a highly regarded historian specializing in Welsh history. Born and raised in Cardiff, Wales, he has a deep passion for exploring the rich cultural and historical heritage of his homeland. Evan has dedicated his academic career to researching and teaching about the fascinating history of Wales.

Education

  • Bachelor of Arts in History from Swansea University
  • Master of Arts in History from Cardiff University
  • Ph.D. in Welsh History from Aberystwyth University
