Biography

Tariro Moyo was born and raised in the vibrant city of Harare, Zimbabwe. She comes from a close-knit family and has always had a deep love for her country and its rich culture. Tariro is known for her warm and friendly personality, always willing to lend a helping hand to her friends and neighbors.

Education Experience

Tariro graduated from the University of Zimbabwe with a Bachelor of Education degree, specializing in primary education. She also holds a teaching certificate in early childhood development, showcasing her commitment to providing quality education to young children.

Work Experience

Tariro has been a primary school teacher for over a decade. She started her teaching career at St. Mary's Primary School right after completing her education, and she has been an integral part of the school's faculty ever since. Her dedication to her students and her innovative teaching methods have earned her the respect of both students and colleagues.

Hobbies

In her free time, Tariro enjoys exploring Zimbabwe's natural beauty. She often goes on hikes and camping trips, taking in the stunning landscapes and wildlife that her country has to offer. She is also an avid reader and enjoys reading novels that explore different cultures and perspectives.
