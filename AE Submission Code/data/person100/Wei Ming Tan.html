About Dr. Wei Ming Tan

Dr. Wei Ming Tan is a highly respected academic and a professor at National Central University. He has a strong passion for teaching and conducting cutting-edge research in the field of computer science, with a particular focus on artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Biography

Dr. Wei Ming Tan was born and raised in Singapore, where he developed a deep appreciation for education from a young age. He holds a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore and completed his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University. His research primarily centers around developing innovative algorithms for real-world applications, particularly in the fields of healthcare and robotics.

Work Experience

Dr. Wei Ming Tan currently serves as an Assistant Professor at National Central University. Prior to his current position, he worked as a Research Scientist at the Institute of Computer Science in Singapore.

Hobbies

Outside of his academic pursuits, Dr. Tan enjoys photography, capturing the beauty of nature and urban landscapes. He is also an accomplished pianist and finds solace in playing the piano in his free time.
