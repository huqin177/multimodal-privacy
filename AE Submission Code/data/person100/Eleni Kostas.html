About Eleni Kostas

Eleni Kostas is a highly respected historian specializing in ancient Greek history. Born and raised in Athens, she developed a passion for history at a young age and pursued her academic interests with dedication.

Education Experience

  • Bachelor's Degree in History from the University of Athens
  • Master's Degree in Ancient Greek History from the University of Thessaloniki
  • Ph.D. in Classical Studies from the University of Ioannina

Work Experience

  • Professor of History at the University of Ioannina, where she has been teaching for over 15 years
  • Published author of several research papers and books on ancient Greek history
  • Served as the department head of the History Department at the University of Ioannina for five years
