Biography:

Aiden Stormrider's fascination with the natural world began in childhood during family camping trips in the rugged mountains of the Pacific Northwest. This love for nature inspired a lifelong journey dedicated to understanding and protecting the environment. Aiden pursued a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science at the University of Washington, followed by a Master of Science in Ecology from Oregon State University. The pursuit of knowledge and a deep connection to the outdoors have been driving forces throughout Aiden's life.

Education Experience:

  • Master of Science in Ecology, Oregon State University
