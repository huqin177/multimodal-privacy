Biography

Juan Carlos Ramirez is a dedicated and passionate individual who was born and raised in Trujillo, Peru. He has always been enthusiastic about education and has pursued a career in academia to contribute to the academic and intellectual growth of his community.

Education

  • Bachelor's Degree in History from National University of Trujillo
  • Master's Degree in Education from National University of Trujillo
  • Ph.D. in History from National University of Trujillo

Work Experience

Juan Carlos Ramirez has been an Assistant Professor at the National University of Trujillo for the past 5 years. He specializes in Latin American history and is known for his engaging teaching style and commitment to his students' success.
