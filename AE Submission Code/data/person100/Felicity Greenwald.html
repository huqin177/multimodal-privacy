Bio

Dr. Felicity Greenwald is an esteemed Affiliate Assistant Professor in History at Rivertown University, where she has been enlightening young minds since 2019. A distinguished scholar in Renaissance Studies, Dr. Greenwald has a passion for unearthing the nuanced lives of historical figures. Her groundbreaking research on the influence of female monarchs on European diplomacy has been published in several acclaimed academic journals. Outside the classroom, Felicity is an avid hiker and a regular at medieval reenactment festivals, where she enjoys bringing history to life for the public. Her students appreciate her dynamic teaching approach which often includes interactive historical simulations.
