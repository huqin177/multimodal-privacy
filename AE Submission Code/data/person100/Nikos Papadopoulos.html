Biography

Nikos Papadopoulos is an accomplished academic with a passion for teaching and research. He was born and raised in Thessaloniki, Greece, and developed a love for learning from an early age. Nikos pursued his higher education at the University of Thessaly, where he earned his Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science. His doctoral research focused on machine learning techniques for natural language processing.
